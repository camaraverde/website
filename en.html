ABOUT CVSM

Innovating project on responsible communication and social responsibility consulting.

Multimedia / CSR Consultors / Infographics

We are an agency with services focused on sustainable development: multimedia (video, web, photography, apps), CSR consultants and certified printed material. We offer our clients to build personalized CSR and communications strategies. In CVSM, all our actions are zero carbono footprint and we are the only mexican agency to deliver our clients a carbon neutral certification. Eventually, between 5 and 10 % of our projects´s cost is directly delivered to NGOs.
